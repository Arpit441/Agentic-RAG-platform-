GlobalTech Solutions - Remote Work Policy (Effective 2026)

1. Eligibility: All full-time employees who have completed their 90-day probationary period are eligible for the hybrid remote work program.
2. Schedule: Employees may work from home up to 3 days per week. Tuesdays and Thursdays are mandatory "core in-office" days for department meetings.
3. Equipment Stipend: GlobalTech provides a one-time $500 stipend for home office equipment (monitors, ergonomic chairs, etc.). This stipend must be expensed through the Concur portal within the first 6 months of employment.
4. Internet Reimbursement: Employees receive a $50 monthly allowance for high-speed internet, automatically added to their paycheck.
5. Working Hours: Core working hours are between 10:00 AM and 3:00 PM in the employee's local time zone. Employees must be available on Slack and Zoom during these hours. Outside of core hours, flexible scheduling is permitted provided the 40-hour weekly requirement is met.
